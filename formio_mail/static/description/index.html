Forms | Mail

Send a Form as PDF-file via mail to everyone you want

Custom mail body and subject and unlimited recipients

Let users and admins easily send Forms to colleagues, family, business partners and anyone else.

Available recipients types:

  • Form components
    Give user the possibility to enter a recipient mail address in a text component or prepare a predefined selection.
  • Partner mail addresses
    Admins can select multiple partner entries (contacts) as recipients.
  • Fixed mail addresses
    Enter one or multiple mail addresses on form builder as recipient. This is great for shared or group mailboxes.
Features & Benefits:
  • Out-of-the-box send a Form as PDF-file to everyone you want.
  • User's can easily specify recipients in the Form.
  • Custom body and subject text for every Form Builder.
  • Open source, so contributions are welcome.
